AUDI RS6-R ABT
A fast wagon
The audi RS6-R abt is a customized version of the Audi RS6. It's known for its impressive speed and handling, making it a popular choice among car enthusiasts.
PORSCHE 911 GTS
The Porsche 911 GTS 992 is a high-performance sports car that combines luxury and speed. It features a powerful engine, precise handling, and a sleek design, making it a standout in the 911 lineup.
Audi R8
The Audi R8 is a luxury sports car known for its sleek design, powerful V10 engine, and exceptional performance. It offers a perfect blend of speed, precision, and comfort.
Almost Italian
The Audi R8 shares about 50% of its parts with the Lamborghini Huracán, including its powerful V10 engine, making it a true supercar with a touch of Italian flair.
LAMBORGHINI HURACAN STO
The Lamborghini Huracán STO (Super Trofeo Omologata) is a track-focused supercar designed for ultimate performance. Inspired by Lamborghini's racing heritage, it features lightweight materials, advanced aerodynamics, and a naturally aspirated V10 engine, delivering an exhilarating driving experience both on and off the track.
PORSCHE GT3 RS
The Porsche 911 GT3 RS 992 is a track-focused sports car. It features advanced aerodynamics, a naturally aspirated flat-six engine, and lightweight materials.
